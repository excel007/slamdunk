SAKURAGI

"I'm a genius!" The red-headed delinquent who turned his raw power into basketball dominance. Known for his unmatched rebounding ability and the legendary dunk that won the final buzzer.

PLAYER STATS

198 HEIGHT (CM)
90 WEIGHT (KG)
10 JERSEY NUMBER
SHOHOKU TEAM

SKILLS & ABILITIES

REBOUNDING

Sakuragi's natural jumping ability and timing make him a rebounding machine. He can out-jump taller opponents and dominate the paint on both offense and defense.

DUNKING

His famous "Rebound King" dunk broke the backboard and defined his explosive style. Sakuragi can dunk from impossible angles with raw power.

QUICK LEARNING

Despite being a beginner, Sakuragi's athletic genius allows him to master complex techniques in days, including the mid-range jump shot.

LEADERSHIP

His intense spirit rallies the team when they need it most. Sakuragi's never-give-up attitude becomes Shohoku's greatest weapon.

KEY MOMENTS

VOLUME 1

I'm a Genius!

Sakuragi joins the Shohoku basketball team to impress Haruko, declaring himself a basketball genius despite knowing nothing about the sport.

VOLUME 15

The Rebound King is Born

During the Shoyo match, Sakuragi's rebounding ability earns him the nickname "Rebound King" and proves his worth to the team.

VOLUME 31

The Final Shot

Sakuragi's game-winning basket against Sannoh cements his legacy as one of the greatest high school basketball players in history.
