TAKEHIKO INOUE

Inoue's hand-drawn kineticism brought a level of realism to Slam Dunk that had never been seen in serialized manga. His commitment to anatomical accuracy and the sheer weight of momentum transformed every dribble into a narrative moment.

"The court is a battlefield of the soul. Every frame must breathe the exhaustion and the ecstasy of the game."

Through meticulous research and a deep-seated love for the sport, Inoue didn't just draw basketball; he captured the very physics of passion.
