SKILLS & ABILITIES

SPEED & QUICKNESS

Miyagi is one of the fastest players in the league. His explosive first step allows him to blow past defenders and create scoring opportunities for teammates.

PASSING VISION

His court awareness and ability to see plays before they develop make him an elite playmaker. Miyagi orchestrates Shohoku's offense with precision and creativity.

DEFENSE

Despite his size, Miyagi is a tenacious defender. His quick hands and lateral speed allow him to stay in front of any opposing guard and create turnovers.

LEADERSHIP & CLUTCH

As the point guard, Miyagi is the team's emotional engine. His energy and confidence never waver, and he's known for stepping up in critical moments.
