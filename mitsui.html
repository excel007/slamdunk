SKILLS & ABILITIES

THREE-POINT SHOOTING

Mitsui is one of the greatest shooters in high school basketball history. His range extends well beyond the arc, and his quick release makes his shot nearly unguardable.

CATCH AND SHOOT

His ability to catch and shoot in one fluid motion allows him to capitalize on the space created by Shohoku's driving players like Rukawa and Sakuragi.

DEFENSE

As the former MVP, Mitsui has excellent defensive fundamentals. His intensity on defense helps Shohoku maintain pressure throughout games.

MENTAL TOUGHNESS

After overcoming personal struggles and injury, Mitsui developed a resilience that allows him to perform under extreme pressure and clutch situations.
