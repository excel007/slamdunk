RUKAWA

The "Super Rookie" with prodigious talent and an ice-cold demeanor. Rukawa's scoring ability is unmatched, but his selfishness must give way to team play for Shohoku to reach the top.

SHOHOKU TEAM

SKILLS & ABILITIES

SCORING

Rukawa is a scoring machine who can score from anywhere on the court. His one-on-one moves are nearly impossible to defend, making him Shohoku's primary offensive weapon.

DRIVE TO THE BASKET

His explosive first step and ability to finish at the rim through contact make him nearly unstoppable in isolation situations.

DEFENSE

Despite his offensive focus, Rukawa is an elite defender. His quick hands and lateral speed allow him to shut down opposing scorers when fully engaged.

CLUTCH PERFORMANCE

Rukawa's focus reaches another level in critical moments. He's known for taking over games in the fourth quarter when the pressure is highest.

KEY MOMENTS
